Clara's Capabilities

Clara The Cat integrates Amazon Alexa skills with our in-house hardware components which consist of more than 10 sensors ready to be used at any given time. It includes:

  • A temperature and humidity sensor
  • A gyroscope
  • An accelerometer
  • A UV sensor
  • A pressure and altitude sensor
  • An IR blaster
  • NFC
  • RFID
  • Bluetooth, WiFi enabled and much more.
